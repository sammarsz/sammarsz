    How It Works

    Our registration process is straightforward and secure. Here's what you need to do:

    1. Fill out the registration form with the required details:
      • Surname
      • First Name
      • Middle Name
      • Date of Birth (DOB)
      • Sex
      • Marital Status (Either: MARRIED, SINGLE, DIVORCED, etc.)
      • Phone Number
      • Email Address
      • Address of Residence
      • City of Residence
      • Local Government Area (LGA) of Residence
      • State of Residence
      • Country of Residence
      • State of Origin
      • LGA of Origin
    2. Review and submit your application.
    3. Our team will review your application and verify your details.
    4. Once verified, you will receive payment instructions.
    5. Follow the payment instructions to complete the registration process.
    6. Wait for the result, which will be available within five (5) to seven (7) working days.
      Note
    • Ensure accurate information to avoid delays.
    • Results will be available within five to seven working days.
