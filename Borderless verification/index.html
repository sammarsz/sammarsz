    FAQ:

    What do I need NIN for ?
      Your National Identity Number (NIN) can be used for a whole lot of things e.g :
    1. Financial Transactions Verification
    2. Educational Resgistrations Verifications
    3. Telecomunication Services Verification
    4. Travel Document Verification
    5. Tax Payment etc..
    What is fresh enrollment?
    This is the process of registering NIN for the first time.
    What do I Need?
      Who can enroll?
    1. Nigerian Citizens
    2. Legal residents
    3. Refugees
    4. Stateless persons
    What is Name modification ?
    Name modification refers to the process of updating or changing the name associated with your NIN
      Reasons for NIN Name Modification
    1. Marriage or Divorce
    2. Change of name due to religion or culture
    3. Error in initial enrollment
    4. Name variation. (e.g nickname to full-name)
    5. Court-ordered name change etc..
    What is DOB modification ?
    Date Of Birth Modification refers to the process of updating or changing the DOB associated with your NIN
      Reasons for DOB Modificatiuon
    1. Error in initial enrollment
    2. Discrepancy in birth certificates on other IDs
    3. Change in birth date due adoption or legal proceedings
    4. Correction of age related errors
    What is BVN Modification?
    BVN Modofication refers to the process of activating and linking your bank generated NIN to NIMC
    How long does it take for my NIN to be ready?
    It takes five (5) to seven (7) working days after payment for your NIN slip to be ready
